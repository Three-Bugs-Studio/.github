## About

Three Bugs Studio is an independent software studio founded by three developers.

We partner with startups, businesses, and creators to design and build scalable digital products—from modern websites to SaaS platforms, internal tools, and AI-powered applications.

We believe that every successful product begins with one thing:

> **A strong foundation.**
